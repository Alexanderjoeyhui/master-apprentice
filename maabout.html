Master & Apprentice is Alexander Joey Hui.
A designer and craftsman based in Newcastle
upon Tyne making fine casual waistcoats by
hand. He uses fabrics sourced from the best
British mills and each product is carefully
designed, cut and constructed

His endeavour is to create clothing for the
modern British gentleman - simple, timeless
and functional tailoring for everyday life.
